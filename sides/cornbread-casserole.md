# Cornbread Casserole

Ingredients:
* 1 can of corn, drained
* 1 can of creamed corn
* 1 cup of sour cream
* 1 stick of melted butter (1/2 cup)
* 1 box of Jiffy Corn Muffin mix

1. Put all of those ingredients in a bowl and mix them up.
1. Transfer the mixture to a parchment paper lined 8Ã—8 casserole pan and bake at 350 degrees for about 45 minutes.
