# Overnight oats

* 1/2 cup oatmeal
* 1 cup milk
* Cinnamon
* 1 tablespoon chia seeds
* 1 dab maple syrup
* Pinch sea salt

Steps:
1. Mix it all together
1. Put in fridge
1. Wait a few hours, and mix it up. Put back in the fridge.

Original Source: https://www.loveandlemons.com/overnight-oats-recipe/

Accoutrement:
* Raisins
* Walnuts
